# 👥 IBM Employee Attrition & Performance Analysis
### Business Analyst Portfolio Project | HR Analytics | Predictive Modeling

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
IBM was experiencing increased employee turnover across departments. 
HR leadership needed to identify **which employees were at risk of leaving** 
and **why** — to implement targeted retention strategies before losing key talent.

## 👥 Stakeholders
| Stakeholder | Interest |
|---|---|
| HR Department | Identify at-risk employees early |
| Operations Management | Reduce hiring & training costs |
| Executive Leadership | Improve workforce stability KPIs |

## 📋 My Role (Business Analyst Perspective)
- Defined the business problem with HR stakeholders
- Gathered data requirements (35 features × 1,500 employee records)
- Identified key KPIs: attrition rate, department risk score, salary band correlation
- Translated model outputs into actionable retention recommendations
- Delivered findings as an executive insight report

## 📊 Key Insights Delivered
- Identified top 5 attrition risk factors: overtime, job satisfaction, monthly income, years at company, work-life balance
- Logistic Regression selected as final model — most interpretable for HR stakeholders (non-technical audience)
- Recommended targeted interventions for high-risk departments

## 🛠️ Tools & Methods
`Python` `Scikit-learn` `Power BI` `Logistic Regression` `Random Forest` `XGBoost` `Correlation Analysis` `Chi-Square Testing`
